Our Heritage Story

For over seven decades, Jodhpur Mishthan has been the epitome of authentic Rajasthani cuisine. Founded in 1950 in the royal city of Jodhpur, we have preserved age-old recipes passed down through generations. Now established in the Pink City of Jaipur, we continue to serve the finest traditional sweets and snacks, made with pure ingredients and crafted with the same passion that started our journey.
